Our Donation Plan

Your donation will help us act quickly during the early hours and days to save lives and provide safe spaces, water, food, shelter and support to children and their families, when and where they need it most.

Food for a family

During a disaster, nonperishable food is always in demand. The most essential products are canned meats, fruits, soups, stews and veggies; peanut butter and jelly; cereal and breakfast bars; bottled water and juices and personal hygiene products.

Shelter for a family

You can provide tents and other essential shelters for families that have lost everything in the event of a catastrophe. Every penny we raise together might give a family a home, and with a home comes a strong sense of new hope.

Education for a family

Children are in a position to continue their education after becoming confident and independent learners. Help improve the prospects for a bright future for tribal children by giving them the opportunity to pursue their education.

You could think of charity as something you do with the extra change you find lying around, or backing a friend running in a local race. This is what we used to feel until we learned what even a small amount of money can do when it is given to the right organizations.

They need us to survive

Regardless of the amount you can afford to donate, your donation counts. Each donation adds up and helps us get down to business and make a positive difference.
